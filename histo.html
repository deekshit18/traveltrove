Heritage Sites

Kerala's heritage sites stand as testament to its rich cultural, historical, and architectural legacy. These landmarks offer a deep dive into the state's ancient traditions, artistic expressions, and architectural marvels. Preserving tales from bygone eras, Kerala's heritage sites are not just tourist attractions but are also symbolic of the state's cultural fabric, offering insights into its storied past and diverse influences. They encapsulate the spirit and essence of Kerala, providing visitors with a holistic understanding of its socio-cultural evolution over the centuries.

Museums

Museums are storehouses of not just our history but also a treasure trove of information regarding the various events and mechanisms that led us to this point in time. They have with them teachings, information and even warnings at times which are of great significance to the future generations. We can learn and observe from those who carved out a place in history. The relics contained inside museums stand testament to the progress we have made over the ages and the direction we're heading in. Our museums have been well-maintained and equipped with certified guides to ensure a seamless and scholarly experience at every single one of them. From Heritage Museums, to Art Museums to niche Museums that catalogue a specific topic, we have safeguarded these testimonials with the best possible efforts and resources. Piligrim Centres

Kerala is known to be amongst the rare places where all major religions have coexisted peacefully for centuries. Each have left their own mark on the culture of the state. You can witness the same by visiting the various religious institutions spread across the state. From Hindu temples, churches and mosques to Jain temples, synagogues and Buddhist monasteries, there are various centers of pilgrimage for our visitors. Do try adding them to your itinerary because they are amongst the most spiritually calming places in the world
